Doha - United Arab Emirates

Country: United Arab Emirates

The best: Being used to Dubai and Abu Dhabi, I've expected Doha to be the same but totally not! Also modern but also some historic places! And everyone is super friendly!

The worst: Not being able to purchase alcohol! Even at my hotel (restaurant and room service) was not possible!

Where to grab a bite: Everywhere where you can eat a lentil soup! “Royal Palace of Buhkari” is a great place!

Resting is important: ”Fraiser Suites Doha” is a very nice 5 star hotel with a great location. If you fly with Qatar Airlines you will get a discount on your stay.

Funny usefull/less stuff: Don’t miss the soek. Great place to walk around, buy some nice things and drink a cup of coffee!
