Lisbon - Portugal

Country: Portugal

The best: The prices!!! It is “cheap” to eat in a restaurant and the food is wonderful!!

The worst: For me it's the dinner time that's too late! A lot of restaurants only open at 7.30/8pm for dinner!!

Where to grab a bite: There are a lot of good places in Lisbon. Fresh fish can be found almost everywhere. Codfish is one of the most typical fishes/dishes and a good place to try it is “D’Bacalhau - Expo”. Delicious!

Resting is important: If you want to stay in the modern part of town, i would suggest “Tivoli Oriente”, which has a great location, indoor pool and nice staff.

Funny usefull/less stuff: A trip to a winery! Near Lisbon are a few wineries and there are some nice tours! And don't forger to go to Belém for the famous “pastéis de belém”.
