Melbourne - Australia

Country: Australia

The best: The people in Melbourne are really easy going. It’s very easy to make some new connections!

The worst: ???

Where to grab a bite: “The bank on Collins” is the place I always visit when I am in Melbourne. I love their Arancini!!!

Resting is important: “Treasury on Collins” was the best hotel for me. Perfect location, great clean rooms, nice breakfast included (with a waffelmaker!) and a nice “wine hour” with one free wine glass and some cheese.

Funny usefull/less stuff: Don’t forget to visit the “South Melbourne Marker” and have a coffee at “Padres”! The Yara Valley Winetour is also a great way to spend your time: great wine and food!!
