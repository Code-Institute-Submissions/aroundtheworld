Dubai - United Arab Emirates

Country: United Arab Emirates

The best: he weather in Dubai can be the best or the worst! It is always hot! I have been there 6/7 times and experienced all the 4 seasons. It doesnt matter: it is always bloody hot! Perfect if you want to go to the beach! And also perfect if you want to go shopping: great airco in the shopping malls!!! The shopping malls are actually one of the main attractions of Dubai: very big and luxurious!

The worst: Dubai can be very expensive. Hotels are not that expensive, even the 5 star ones, but restaurants are pretty expensive. Going to the supermarket can be cheaper but when you are on holidays you want to go out and try the traditional food and then you will pay a lot in the restaurants. Wine is very expensive also.
